Business can benefit from managing waste more effectively. Neehoy is working with organisations in both the public and private sector to develop "cradle to grave" technology solutions.

We are currently working with Nottingham Energy Partnership (NEP) to develop a solution for organisations. NEP and Neehoy work in partnership to provide a fully integrated service starting with sustainable procurement policy and process development through to asset management, staff training and tool. The partnership is supported by Healthcare and Bioscience iNet and Medilink East Midlands Ltd and part financed by the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF).

We can work with your business throughout the product lifecycle starting with procurement through to asset management, and finally disposal.
